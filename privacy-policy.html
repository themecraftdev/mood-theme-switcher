Privacy Policy
Mood Theme Switcher

Last updated: June 3, 2026

Overview

Mood Theme Switcher is a Microsoft Edge new tab extension that displays a clock, current weather, and quick links. This policy explains what data is used and how.

We are committed to your privacy. This extension does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

Data We Do Not Collect

This extension does not:

  • Collect or store any personal information
  • Track your browsing history or activity
  • Use cookies or analytics
  • Share any data with the extension developer
  • Send any data to our own servers

Third-Party API Usage

To display local weather, the extension contacts two external services:

ip-api.com — Used to determine your approximate city/region from your IP address, solely to fetch relevant weather. Your IP address is sent to ip-api.com as part of a standard HTTP request. We do not store this data. See their privacy policy at ip-api.com/docs/legal.

OpenWeatherMap (api.openweathermap.org) — Used to fetch current weather conditions for your location. Only your approximate city coordinates (derived from ip-api) are sent. We do not store this data. See their privacy policy at openweather.co.uk/privacy-policy.

These requests happen locally in your browser. The extension developer has no access to this data.

Local Storage

The extension uses your browser's local storage solely to save your preferences (e.g. chosen theme or quick links). This data never leaves your device.

Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

  • storage — to save your local preferences
  • alarms — to refresh weather data periodically
  • network access — to contact ip-api.com and OpenWeatherMap

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new "Last updated" date.
